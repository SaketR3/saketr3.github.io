Hi! I'm currently a student at the University of Illinois - Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), and will be **applying to PhD programs this fall focusing on AI alignment**! 

I am interested in improving **alignment, RLHF, and safety** in **high-variance reward landscapes** such as pluralistic alignment, dual-use safety dilemmas, visual ambiguity, and cross-cultural alignment. I am especially interested in improving performance in these domains by **building better reward models** and **integrating more internal model information** such as loss landscape curvature into **post-training processes**.
